Stories Behind the Canvas

Abbas Batliwala

Abbas Batliwala, a highly experienced and renowned artist born in 1958 in Udaipur, Rajasthan, is known for his unique style featuring captivating figurative paintings with expressive big eyes. His artistic journey began after completing his education in drawing and painting at Udaipur University in 1982. Since then, Abbas has continuously showcased his creativity on canvas with oil, earning numerous awards and accolades. Abbas Batliwala's artwork beautifully portrays the essence of Indian rural culture and everyday life. Each painting captures the emotions and charm of the subjects with its distinctive big eyes, which may appear slightly intimidating at first glance but are actually endearing. His captivating collection of paintings can be found in prestigious galleries such as the National Gallery of Modern Art, Lalit Kala Akademi, Jawahar Kala Kendra, and many more across India and abroad. If you're looking for a valuable addition to your art collection, consider investing in a painting by Abbas Batliwala. Owning at least one of his artworks is truly worth it, as they hold immense artistic and emotional value. .

Anjana Datta

Ananjana Datta is an accomplished artist renowned for her evocative and thought-provoking works that blend traditional techniques with contemporary themes. Her art often explores the nuances of identity, culture, and the human experience, inviting viewers to reflect on their own connections to these themes. Datta's signature style is marked by vibrant colors, intricate patterns, and a keen attention to detail, which together create a rich tapestry of visual storytelling. Her pieces resonate with emotional depth, often drawing inspiration from personal experiences and cultural narratives. In addition to her creative endeavors, Ananjana Datta is committed to community engagement and art education. She frequently participates in workshops and exhibitions, sharing her insights and techniques with aspiring artists. Through her work and outreach, Datta not only enriches the art community but also fosters a greater appreciation for the power of artistic expression in shaping cultural conversations. Her contributions make her a significant figure in the contemporary art landscape, inspiring others to explore their own artistic voices.
